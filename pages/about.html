about me.


Hello, my name is Takoda and I am twenty three years old. I am from the United States, and was born in New York but lived in New Jersey for most of my life. I have always been interested in creativity, psychology, organization, and expression. These interests are why I chose to study in the New Media Design program, as User Experience and Graphic Design perfectly combines all my interests. Moving to Sweden for this program was my first time leaving the United States, and I hope to continue traveling around to experience various cultures while also developing valuable skills. I enjoy the fall atmosphere, so October&November is my favorite time of the year. I enjoy highly desaturated design in images and videos, it gives a nostalgic feel. I never get tired of seeing well done melancholic inducing designs, this is probably why I like Sweden. I also really like video games and reading, lately I've been playing Overwatch 2 but some of my favorite games/series are: The Elder Scrolls, The Witcher, Halo, Fallout, and way more that I won't name otherwise I'd be here all day.






about the city

I chose to study in Europe because I wanted to live in a place where safe & clean walkable cities with reliable public transporation exist. Jönköping fulfills everything I could want from a city, well almost everything. Our food in America is much better than the options here... Speaking of food here, my favorite restaurant in the city is a Thai restaurant near the train station called 9 Thai. Also, there is a very nice vantage point that overlooks the city if you decide to go on a short walk up a hill, it's peaceful up there, but I've only gone there one time because the hill is steep and I'm not here to excercise. I can't tell you any more about the city because I'm a hermit that never leaves my room, but I'm sure it's nice out there.
